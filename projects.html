Emergency Alert System

Once a fall is detected, the app instantly triggers its emergency response system without requiring any user action. It automatically sends alerts to pre-saved emergency contacts, notifying them of the situation in real time. These alerts contain clear warning messages and can optionally include the user’s current GPS location, making it easier for caregivers, family members, or emergency services to reach the user quickly. The system may also continue sending follow-up notifications if there is no response, ensuring that help is not delayed. This fast, automated communication significantly reduces response time and increases the likelihood of timely medical assistance and safety.

Automatic Fall Detection

The app leverages built-in mobile sensors such as the accelerometer and gyroscope to continuously monitor and analyze user movement patterns in real time. It accurately tracks motion, orientation, and speed to understand normal daily activities and identify unusual behavior. When a sudden impact, rapid movement change, or abnormal body position is detected, the system closely observes the user’s activity level. If this event is followed by a period of inactivity or lack of movement, the app intelligently classifies it as a potential fall. This entire process happens automatically without requiring any manual input from the user, ensuring fast, reliable, and hands free emergency detection while maintaining uninterrupted safety and peace of mind.

Real Time Monitoring

The system works silently and continuously in the background, constantly analyzing motion, posture, and activity patterns using the phone’s built-in sensors. It ensures uninterrupted protection without affecting performance, battery life, or the user’s normal phone usage. The app intelligently distinguishes between regular movements and potential emergencies, such as sudden falls or prolonged inactivity. When abnormal activity is detected, the system responds instantly by triggering alerts, requesting user confirmation, and notifying emergency contacts if necessary. This guarantees 24/7 safety coverage, giving both users and their families complete peace of mind.
